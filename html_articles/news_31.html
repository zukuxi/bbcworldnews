Spanish and Argentinian fans react outside World Cup final

Ecstatic Spanish spectators and disappointed Argentine fans spoke to the BBC outside New York New Jersey Stadium after the World Cup final on Sunday.

Argentina played extra time with only 10 men when Spain's Ferran Torres scored the winning goal after 106 minutes.

New video shows man believed to be Tyler Robinson before Charlie Kirk shooting

Prosecutors on Tuesday released new surveillance footage that they say shows Tyler Robinson, the man accused of killing Charlie Kirk.

Agent David Hull described the footage, which appeared to show Robinson driving a vehicle and walking through a parking garage.

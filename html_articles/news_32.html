Dramatic video shows wildfire surround freight train in Canada

Video captured on 13 July shows a wildfire surrounding a freight train in Ontario, Canada. CN Rail told the BBC that it temporarily suspended operations in the area and relocated employees. The workers seen in the video were able to escape and are safe. Currently, there are multiple wildfires burning across the northwestern part of the province, with numerous fires remaining out of control due to extreme heat, dry conditions, and strong winds.

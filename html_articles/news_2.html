People flee as building collapses on Caracas outskirts

Back-to-back earthquakes have hit Venezuela, leaving at least 160 people dead, the country's acting President Delcy Rodríguez has said.

The capital Caracas and its surrounding areas have seen some of the worst damage.

In the area of El Junquito, on the outskirts of Caracas, a content creator has captured the aftermath of a building collapsing.

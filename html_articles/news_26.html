Bodycam footage shows airlift of girl stranded by Texas floods

Bodycam footage showed the moment a rescuer reached a family of three, and their dogs, sheltering in their destroyed house as water flooded parts of Uvalde county, Texas.

The rescuer airlifted a girl to safety, telling the rest of the family that another helicopter was on the way to take them.

At least two people have died in flooding across central Texas - the same area that saw more than 130 deaths in flash floods last summer.

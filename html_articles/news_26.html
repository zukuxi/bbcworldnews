Watch: At the scene where Bangkok's deadly bar fire unfolded

The BBC's South East Asia correspondent Jonathan Head has visited the site where at least 27 people were killed during a bar fire in Thailand.

The deadly blaze broke out at Rong Beer Na Lat Phrao – the local name of the bar – which is situated in the Chatuchak district of Bangkok.
